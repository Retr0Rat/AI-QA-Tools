## Overview
This course introduces students to artificial intelligence concepts, history, and modern applications. Students examine AI through multiple lenses including machine learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

## Topics
- History of AI
- AI subfields and paradigms
- Industry applications of AI
- Current challenges in AI
- Future directions and research areas

## Tools
- Python
- Jupyter Notebooks

## Projects
- AI industry analysis report
- Exploration of real-world AI use cases
