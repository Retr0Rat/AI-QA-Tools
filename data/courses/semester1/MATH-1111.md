## Overview
This course builds the mathematical intuition and skills needed to understand and implement AI algorithms. Every topic is connected directly to its application in machine learning.

## Topics
- Linear algebra (vectors, matrices, transformations)
- Calculus and gradients
- Probability and distributions
- Statistics and inference
- Applications to ML algorithms

## Tools
- Python (NumPy)
- Jupyter Notebooks

## Projects
- Mathematical foundations assignments
- Applied statistics exercises
